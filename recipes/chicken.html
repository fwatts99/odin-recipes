Slow Cooker Chicken Recipe

Description:

This slow cooker chicken recipe is perfect for a busy weeknight dinner. Just set it and forget it!

Ingredients:
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Steps:
  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix the chicken broth, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken.
  4. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
  5. Serve with your favorite sides.
