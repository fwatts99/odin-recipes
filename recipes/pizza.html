Pizza Recipe

Description:

This delicious pizza recipe is perfect for a family night in. Customize it with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients:
  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Steps:
  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add pepperoni, bell peppers, and any other desired toppings.
  7. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and oregano on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
